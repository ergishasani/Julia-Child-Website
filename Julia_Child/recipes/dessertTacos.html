Dessert Tacos

Course

Total Time

Servings

Dessert

15 minutes

8 slices

Scroll Down

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup white granulated sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. cinnamon
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 pt. vanilla ice cream
  • chocolate syrup
  • chocolate sprinkles

Instructions

  • In a large shallow dish mix together the granulated sugar and the cinnamon. Set to the side.
  • In a large skillet heat the canola oil until hot.
  • Place one tortilla in the skillet cook for 10 seconds then using tongs flip over.
  • Remove from the skillet, drain quickly on a paper towel. Dip both sides in the cinnamon sugar.
  • Shake excess cinnamon sugar off and quickly fold while tortilla is still warm.
  • epeat until all the taco shells are cooked.
  • Once the taco shells are cooked, filled tacos with 1-2 small scoops of vanilla ice cream, drizzle with chocolate syrup and top with sliced strawberries.
  • Sprinkle with chocolate sprinkles. Serve quickly.
