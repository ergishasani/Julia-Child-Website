Crepes Filled with Banana
Hazelnut Cream and Nuts

Course

Baking Time

Total Time

Servings

Breakfast & Branch

5 minutes

20 minutes

8 people

Scroll Down

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Bisquick™ Original baking mix
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 bananas, thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup hazelnut cream
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Chocolate Syrup

Julia Child’s Special Crepes Filled with Banana, Hazelnut Cream and Nuts

Instructions

  1. Beat the egg and mix the milk, Bisquick, cardamom, sugar and salt in a bowl.
  2. In a saucepan or crepe maker, add 1/4 of the mixture to the saucepan until the surface is completely covered. Once ready, flip over and remove. You can start stacking them on a dish or preparing them little by little before serving.
  3. Once the crepes are ready, add a spoonful of hazelnut cream, a few slices of banana and half a teaspoon of nuts.
  4. Fold the crepes in half and then again down the middle. Drizzle some chocolate syrup and sprinkle a few nuts.
