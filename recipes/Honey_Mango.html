Special Honey-Mango Granola Cups

Total Time

Servings

12 people

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups Cascadian Farm™ organic oats & honey granola (from 16-oz box)n
  • 3 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/3 cup full-fat plain Greek yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons finely diced fresh mango
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated lime peel

Julia Child’s Special Honey-Mango Granola Cups

Get a taste of the tropics in these bite-sized cups made with bits of mango and organic oats & honey granola held together with sweet honey. The best part? You can make them ahead and have breakfast or a post-workout snack all week long.

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350°F. Spray 12 mini muffin cups with cooking spray.
  2. In food processor, place granola. Cover; process until coarsely crumbled.
  3. Spoon 1 heaping tablespoon of the mixture into each muffin cup. Press mixture down in center and up sides of muffin cups
